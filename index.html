Info:

This is a social running group for like minded people. We cater for athletes of all levels, whether you run marathons or are doing couch to 5k come on down and meet us.
We meet on Wednesday evening at 6:30pm at Science World. 5.5km and 10km available.
We also meet on Sunday Morning at 10am at Second Beach Concession stand. 6.5km and 10km available
We would like to add that we accommodate anyone that would like to walk these routes too

Rules:

This running club exists purely to offer an opportunity for like-minded runners to exercise, run and socialise together.

All attendees should consider this group as an informal gathering of people who will run together in a loosely structured format.

The group does not take responsibility for any injury, loss or damage that might arise as a result of participation in group activities.

We're all in this together to create a welcoming environment. Let's treat everyone with respect. Healthy debates are natural, but kindness is required.
